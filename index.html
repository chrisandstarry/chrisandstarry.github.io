Christopher David Rowe & Yeasl Kim
joyfully invite you to share in their celebration of love and commitment.

Ceremony

The ceremony commences at two thirty in the afternoon at Lake Cathie, within the Lakeside Woods Estate. To be followed by some light refreshments at Chris’ parent’s house.

The ceremony is a 5 hour drive from Sydney but there will be plenty of places to park. If you are coming by train make sure someone can pick you up. Let us know if you need help (before the day!)

If you have a spare seat, let us know too! We ask you to arrive at Chris’ parent’s house at 66 Kenwood Drive Lake Cathie at two o’clock sharp. At two fifteen, Chris himself will be leading the guests down to the ceremony at the water’s edge by way of a short bushwalk.

Guests are encouraged to dress creatively and comfortably. Please keep in mind that we are planning an outdoor ceremony. High heels and uncomfortable formal wear are not recommended.

Reception

The ceremony will be followed by an evening of food, candy, music, laughter and awkward but enthusiastic dancing.

Please join us at the Town Green Inn, Port Macquarie at six o’clock.

The reception is a 20 minute drive from the ceremony and we are planning to car pool where possible

The address is 4 Horton Street, which is right in the heart of Port Macquarie so parking may be a little more difficult.

Gifts

The very best present we can receive is your presence at our wedding and your love and support as we embark on this adventure together. We recognise that especially for those of you coming from far away; simply attending our wedding will already entail spending money on travel and hotel accommodations.

We have set up a wishing well and a guest book for guests who wish to leave cards and messages. Many of you are contributing your time and energy to this event, and for the gift of your participation we thank you.

Accommodation

Let us know if you need any help with accommodation for the evening. We have booked a number of cabins in Sundowner Caravan Park which is right behind the reception venue, so feel free to ask if you would like one.

Food

If you have any dietary requirements, please let us know at least two weeks before the wedding. We also have a private bar at the reception, available for drinks purchases. Tea and coffee will also be provided.
